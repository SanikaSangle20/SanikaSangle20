⋄About kokum

n Konkani, kokum is known as Bhirand (बिरिंड). It's a fruit-bearing plant native to the western coast of India particularly the Konkan region. The dried peel of the kokum fruit is a common ingredient in Konkani cuisine, used for its souring and digestive properties. Kokum is also valued for its medicinal properties, like its ability to relieve indigestion and promote overall well-being.
