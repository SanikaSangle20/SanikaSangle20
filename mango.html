♦About Mango

The Konkan region of Maharashtra, particularly coastal areas like Ratnagiri, Rajapur, and Devgad, is renowned for producing high-quality Alphonso mangoes. These mangoes are grown in mineral-rich red soil, considered to yield the best Alphonso mangoes globally. The Alphonso, also known as "Hapus," is a seasonal fruit, harvested from late March through June.
